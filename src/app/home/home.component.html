Aerospace Engineering

New problems require new solutions, and old problems need fresh perspective. SAPANA is a student-based research consultancy firm offering expert insights into current and future problems relating to space. Our expertise spans aerospace and mechanical engineering, political science, economics, computer science, and business. We are independent, inquisitive, and dedicated to excellence.


Based in Colorado, we are embedded in an unparalleled network of space experts from government, industry, and research environments. Our goal is to support the sustainable development of space for the good of all humankind.


Our name is inspired by the Native American story of a young girl named Sapana who dreamed of climbing a tree and reaching the sky. We chose this name to inspire us to reach to heights unknown.
